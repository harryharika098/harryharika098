first elephant

After months of anticipation, summer is just around the corner. Whether you’ll be relaxing on a beach begins with a young boy falling from his window in the middle of the night. His family watches him from his hospital room, where he lies in a coma. While his mother waits by his side, refusing to speak to anyone, the family’s friends and neighbors wait back home, each confronting their own role in the events that led up to the tragedy.

second elephant

Over the course of one week, the mothers in each of these four families are forced to face their own secrets and the uncomfortable truths that connect them to one another all while anxiously awaiting the fate of the young boy. This novel faces uncomfortable truths of its own: intuitions we ignore, the sacrifices of motherhood and what happens when we put our needs ahead of our children’s.,
